The Problem

A Good Listing and a High-Performing One Are Very Different Things.

Most hosts know something isn't working. Occupancy is inconsistent, the nightly rate feels like a guess, and the photos are fine, but not special. The gap between where you are and where top-performing listings sit in your market is rarely one big problem. It's a handful of fixable ones. The audit finds them.

The Analysis

Three Layers of Analysis. One Clear Report.

We pull data from three sources and run it through our AI pipeline:

Layer 01

Your Listing Photos

Gemini AI analyses up to 16 images across 7 design dimensions, scoring your property on what actually drives guest decisions.

Photo quality · Furniture tier · Interior style · Colour sophistication · Space optimisation · Lighting · Styling details

Layer 02

Guest Reviews & Listing Content

We assess your written listing, amenities, and guest feedback to identify gaps in how your property is presented and perceived, and what guests are actually responding to.

Layer 03

Your Market

We pull live ADR, occupancy rates, and RevPAR data for comparable listings in your area, and benchmark your property against the peer median, so you can see exactly where the gap is.

Powered by AirROI market data

The Report

A Branded Report With Numbers Behind Every Recommendation.

01

Design Score

A composite score out of 100 across 7 design dimensions, with a breakdown by room and category. You'll see exactly where your property sits, and what's dragging the score down.

02

Design Premium Index

How your listing's RevPAR compares to the peer median in your market. A DPI below 1.0 means you're earning less than comparable properties. The report shows you the gap in £ terms.

03

Revenue Uplift Projection

Three scenarios, conservative, expected, and optimistic, showing the estimated £ annual gain from design improvements, plus a payback period so you can weigh the investment.

e.g. "Expected uplift: £3,200/year. Payback period: 4 months."

04

Ranked Recommendations

Every recommendation scored High / Medium / Low ROI, with estimated cost range and implementation time. You know exactly where to spend first to move the needle fastest.

Questions

Good Questions. Quick Answers.

Is it really free?

Yes. No card required, no catch. We offer the audit because it starts the right conversation.

How long does it take to receive?

We aim to deliver within one business day. Every report is reviewed by our team before it's sent.

What do I need to submit?

Your Airbnb listing URL and a few property details. The system does the rest, it scrapes your listing, pulls your market data, and runs the analysis automatically.
