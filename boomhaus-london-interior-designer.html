Who We Work With

Interior Design That Earns Its Keep.

BoomHaus is a London interior design studio with a difference: we measure our work in revenue. Every design decision we make is tested against a single question, does this help our client charge more, fill more nights, or retain tenants longer?

We work across three markets: short-let and Airbnb properties, build-to-rent and serviced apartments, and residential homes. Our approach is the same across all three: considered design, professionally specified materials, and a process built around your timeline.
