Our Approach

The Same Rigour. A Completely Different Brief.

BoomHaus was built on the principle that good design should pay back. In commercial properties, that means occupancy rates and ADR. In a home, it means spaces that work harder, feel better, and hold their value longer.

We don't design for photoshoots. We design for how you live — your morning routine, your dinner parties, the way you actually use your kitchen. The difference shows in every room we deliver.

Every residential project starts with a proper brief, not a mood board. We ask the questions that most designers skip, because getting those answers right is what turns a well-furnished room into a space you genuinely love.

We don't design for photoshoots. We design for the life that happens inside the frame.
— BoomHaus Design Philosophy

How It Works

Simple from the Start.

01

We Have a Conversation

Tell us about your home, your brief, and your budget. There's no commitment at this stage — just a conversation to see if we're the right fit. Most first calls are 20 minutes.

02

We Send a Proposal

A fixed-scope proposal with a clear fee, timeline, and exactly what's included. No hourly billing surprises. You know what you're getting before you commit to anything.

03

We Deliver the Design

Concept, specification, procurement, and installation — all handled by us. You're involved in the decisions that matter, and spared the ones that don't.

Why BoomHaus for Your Home.

We come from a commercial background — which means we think carefully about value, durability, and return on investment. Those instincts don't switch off when the brief is personal.

01

Commercial Rigour, Personal Result

Every specification decision we make is filtered through quality, longevity, and value. You get the discipline of a commercial brief with a result that feels entirely personal.

02

Honest, Fixed-Fee Process

No hourly billing. No hidden sourcing fees. No surprise invoices. We agree a scope, agree a price, and deliver to it. Your budget is a constraint we design within — not around.

03

Designed to Last

We specify for the long term. The pieces we choose are built to survive real life — children, pets, dinner parties, years of use. We'd rather steer you right than sell you something beautiful that breaks.
