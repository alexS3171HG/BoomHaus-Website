The Design

What Makes This Pack Different.

The Take Five is built for the operator who understands that "good enough" is invisible in today's market. Rich velvets, solid white oak, wool rugs, brass and marble accents, every material has been chosen for its tactile quality and lasting appeal. The design philosophy is confident understatement: a soft neutral base layered with warm greens, creams, and statement oak pieces, finished with oversized lighting and carefully placed detail. The result is a scroll-stopping, review-generating interior that commands and sustains a premium rate.
