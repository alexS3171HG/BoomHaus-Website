What Makes This Pack Different.

The BTR pack is a fully specified, professionally specified fit-out that combines timeless, expressive design with rigorous commercial strategy. Built for the operator who refuses to settle for generic interiors. It delivers "Soho House aesthetics" without the institutional price tag, expressive, people-first spaces that feel like a relaxing home, not a showroom. Available in 1, 2 and 3-bedroom configurations, making it the most scalable pack in the range.
