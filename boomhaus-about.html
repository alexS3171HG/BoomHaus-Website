Where We Started

Built from a Specific Frustration.

BoomHaus was born from watching property owners bleed revenue while trying to furnish spaces that ended up looking generic and taking far too long to get live. Every empty day is money you never recover. Traditional interior design didn't understand that, or didn't care.

Our founders weren't designers who moved into short-lets. They were short-let operators who learned to design. That distinction matters. We've sat on your side of the table. We know what drives occupancy, what triggers the algorithm, and what actually earns a five-star review.

We built BoomHaus to be the partner we wished we'd had, one that moves fast, specifies smart, and never loses sight of the commercial reality your property has to survive.

Operators first. Designers second.
The founding principle behind everything we build.
How We Think

Three Principles. Every Project.

01

People Before Properties

We design for the people who use the space, guests who need to feel at home, clients who need to feel confident, and suppliers who need to feel valued. Good design serves the people inside it, not just the camera pointed at it.

02

Tech That Creates Space to Think

We use smart systems, automation, and AI, not to replace design thinking, but to free it up. By automating the logistics and admin that slow traditional studios down, our team spends its time on what actually matters: creative decisions and real relationships.

03

Small Projects. Long Partnerships.

We don't treat your first unit as a one-off transaction. We treat it as the beginning of something bigger. Our goal isn't to sell you furniture today, it's to be the partner you call when you're ready for your fifth property, or your fiftieth.
