The Design

What Makes This Pack Different.

The Classic is the premium standard for properties where warmth, character, and timeless quality are the brief. Acacia wood, linen, solid oak, travertine — every material is chosen for longevity and aesthetic staying power. This is the pack for the host who understands that the right interior is an insurance policy against void periods: in a competitive market, this design-led fit-out ensures your property is the first to be booked and commands the highest achievable rates.
