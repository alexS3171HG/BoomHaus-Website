The Challenge

BTR Design Is Either Too Corporate or Too Expensive.

Build-to-rent operators face a specific problem: the budget hotel aesthetic that once passed for “design” no longer competes. Modern renters expect something that feels considered, personal, and worth the premium price.

At the same time, the bespoke interior design route is slow, expensive, and hard to replicate across multiple units. BoomHaus is designed specifically for this gap, built to professional specification, consistent across every unit, at a price point that makes the ROI clear.

The BTR Pack

Designed Specifically for City Operators.

The BTR pack uses a clean grey and mango wood palette with brass accents, professional enough for corporate renters, warm enough to feel like a home. Available in 1-bed, 2-bed, and 3-bed (POA) configurations.

Every item is professionally specified. The upholstery, the finishes, the hardware, all selected to hold up to the churn of multiple tenants. You won’t be replacing pieces after 18 months.
